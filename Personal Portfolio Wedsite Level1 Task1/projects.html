MY PROJECTS


1.IBM Z Day Datathon

In the IBM Z Day Datathon event, our team embarked on an innovative project aimed at revolutionizing the startup ecosystem by harnessing the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI). Our goal was to create a dynamic and efficient online platform that would serve as a bridge between ambitious startup entrepreneurs and prospective investors. The centerpiece of our project is a sophisticated AI-driven website designed to streamline the often intricate process of connecting startups with investors. Leveraging the robust capabilities of IBM Z technology, we developed an intelligent matchmaking algorithm that analyzes various parameters, such as industry, business model, and growth potential, to facilitate meaningful and mutually beneficial connections.

Key Features:

Smart Profiling System: Our platform employs AI algorithms to create comprehensive profiles for both startup entrepreneurs and investors. These profiles go beyond basic information, delving into the core values, goals, and preferences of each participant. Dynamic Matchmaking Algorithm: The heart of our project lies in an advanced AI-driven matchmaking algorithm that considers a multitude of factors to pair startups with investors who align with their vision and objectives. This dynamic system ensures a higher likelihood of successful and compatible partnerships. Real-time Collaboration Tools: To enhance communication and collaboration, we integrated real-time messaging, video conferencing, and document sharing features directly within the platform. This facilitates efficient interaction between entrepreneurs and investors, fostering a seamless connection-building process. Data Security and Privacy: Recognizing the importance of safeguarding sensitive information, we prioritized robust security measures, utilizing IBM Z's trusted capabilities to ensure the highest standards of data protection and privacy. User-Friendly Interface: The platform boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface, ensuring that both startup entrepreneurs and investors can navigate effortlessly through the website. This design focus aims to enhance the overall user experience and encourage active participation.

Impact and Future Prospects:

Our AI-driven platform has the potential to significantly transform the startup-investor matchmaking landscape, fostering innovation and growth within the entrepreneurial community. By participating in the IBM Z Day Datathon, we not only demonstrated the capabilities of AI in solving real-world challenges but also paved the way for future advancements in connecting diverse stakeholders within the business ecosystem. Our project aligns with the ethos of leveraging cutting-edge technology for positive societal impact, and we look forward to further refining and expanding our platform in collaboration with the broader entrepreneurial and investor communities.


2.Calculator Website

For my latest project, I undertook the task of creating a fully functional and aesthetically pleasing calculator website using HTML and CSS. The primary objective was to develop a user-friendly interface that seamlessly integrates form and function, catering to both the practical needs of a calculator and the visual preferences of users.

This project highlights the versatility of HTML and CSS in creating functional and visually appealing web applications. The calculator website serves as a practical tool for users while showcasing the importance of design in enhancing user engagement. As I continue to refine this project, future developments may include additional scientific calculator functions, improved styling features, and potential integration with JavaScript for enhanced interactivity. This endeavor underscores the power of HTML and CSS in crafting efficient and stylish web applications tailored to user needs.
